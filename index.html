Founders

Mimerse started as a research idea at Stockholm University and the realization that for digital interventions to succeed they need to be built for it. Far to many great sollutions never leave the lab or psychology departments. Mimerse was funded in the summer of 2014 by the brothers William and Alexander Hamilton. Since then the team has expanded.

Vision

To play an instrumental part in the transformation of clinical treatment and shift towards VR-based interventions.

Products

Our first product is a software that will automatically treat various forms of common phobias. It will be sold and contain features both for clinicians and consumers.
